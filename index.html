Is the generated signature legally valid?

Digital signatures created with our tool can be used for many personal and business documents. However, the legal validity of a digital signature depends on the laws in your jurisdiction and the specific use case.

Absolutely. Once you download your digital signature, you can use it for signing PDFs, online forms, and various other digital documents where electronic signatures are accepted.
